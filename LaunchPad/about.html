Hey! I am Chris Kim, a freshman CS student(c/o 2025).


BoilerChad is the health related website that covers the fields of diet, workingout, and lifting.
This project is primarily motivated from a lot of Boilermakers who commit large amount of their time at the COREC, exercising, working out,
and all other activities related to the physical activities. With this being said, this website would guide the user on several topics, as previously mentioned.
The user can choose a specific portal to go to based on what they are looking for, and based on their input information
(ex: I want to [gain/lose] weights by [user input] lbs within [user input] period of time. OR I want to work out for [user input] times/week. Generate the workout plan.),
the calculator would generate the corresponding outputs.
